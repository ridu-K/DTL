Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are some good resources for learning bivariate statistics?

    Check our resources section.

  • Can I learn bivariate statistics without a strong math background?

    Yes, of course.

  • What types of data are best suited for bivariate analysis?

    Bivariate analysis is typically used with quantitative data (numerical values). However, it can also be applied to categorical data (data with distinct categories) in some cases.

  • What are the prerequisites for studying bivariate statistics?

    A basic understanding of algebra and data analysis concepts would be beneficial. However, most resources introduce the necessary math within the context of bivariate statistics.
